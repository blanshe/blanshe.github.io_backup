Birth Rate Falling...Where?

Data shows that birth rate is falling among American Indian Alaska natives than any other racial group.

By MUSINGUZI BLANSHE

Published September 9, 2019

Between 1980 and 2015, birth rate among American Indian Alaska natives fell from 20.7 to 9.7 per 1000 women, a staggering 90.3 percent reduction.
